Price Updater

Nightly price sync for sealed (TCGplayer scrape), graded slabs (Scrydex live comps), and raw cards (scrydex_price_cache). All three auto-raise to follow the market and flag drops for review.
Run dashboards
Sealed Runs
Per-run review of dailyrunner.py's TCGplayer scrape. Per-row Apply / Dismiss / Block, plus the bulk-approve form for small charm-rounding drops.
Slab Runs
Per-slab Scrydex live comp comparison. Auto-raises in Shopify, flags overpriced slabs for human review.
Raw Runs
Cache-driven raw-card pricing grouped by print. Apply per-group, dismiss, or block.
Tools
Big Movers
Auto-applied price changes from each updater's latest run, filtered by |Δ%|. One-click Block on anything that looks like data corruption.
Raw Rebind
Manual scrydex rebind for in-stock raw_cards the nightly updater can't price (variant or set name mismatch).
Slab TCG Backfill
Match Shopify slab listings to TCGplayer IDs so the nightly slab updater can price them.
Price Blocks
Cards / variants the nightly updaters skip on every run. Permanent until removed here.
Live + manual
Live Log
Streaming output of the most recent dailyrunner invocation.
Inventory
Inventory reconciliation views (legacy sub-routes).
